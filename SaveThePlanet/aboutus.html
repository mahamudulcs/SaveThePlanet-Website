About Us

Allan, Mahamudul, Miranda & Yong

Our mission for this project is to make an interactive webpage to spread awareness about global warming and and climate change issues around the world. Through the interactive map, you will be able to explore different countries on the globe and the specfic climate change issues associated with different locations. This is an open source project. We welcome anyone with their expertise may contribute to the project in the future.
